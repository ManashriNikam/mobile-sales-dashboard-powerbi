**Project Overview**

This project presents an interactive Mobile Sales Dashboard developed using Power BI to analyze sales performance, revenue trends, product performance, and regional insights.
The dashboard helps in understanding business growth, identifying top-performing products, and making data-driven decisions.

**Objectives**

- Analyze total sales revenue
- Track monthly and yearly sales trends
- Identify top-selling mobile brands and models
- Compare regional sales performance
- Analyze profit margins and customer trends

**Key Dashboard Insights**
  
- Total Revenue & Total Units Sold
-  Region-wise Sales Distribution
-  Brand-wise Performance Analysis
-  Monthly Sales Trend Line Chart
-  Profit Analysis
-  Interactive Filters (Slicers)

**Tools & Technologies Used**

- Power BI
- Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query)
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling
